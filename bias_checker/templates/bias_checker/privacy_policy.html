Privacy Policy for Document Files Gender Checker

Thank you for using the Document Files Gender Checker. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting any personal information you may share when using our tool.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal or document data submitted through the Gender Checker tool. When you upload a document, it is processed temporarily in memory solely for the purpose of gender bias analysis and is not retained after the session ends.

How Your Data is Used

  • Uploaded DOCX files are not saved, stored, or shared.
  • Files are processed locally on our server to identify gender-biased language.
  • After processing, the modified document is made available for immediate download.
  • No version of the original or edited document is retained.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your information or uploaded documents with third parties. All document processing is handled internally and securely.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your usage or store personal data.

Data Security

Although no documents are stored, we implement standard security measures to protect our platform and its users during active document processing. All uploads are handled over secure (HTTPS) connections.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
